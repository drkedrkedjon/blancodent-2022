Our services.

Implantology.

Permanently recover the function and aesthetics of your teeth, implanting small pieces of metal, and you can live without problems as if it were your own teeth. At Blancodent, we use the latest German technology to provide you with implant placement with virtually no surgery, discomfort or pain.

More about implants...

Orthodontics.

Orthodontics is the specialty of dentistry aimed at correcting incorrectly positioned teeth. When the teeth are not aligned and as a result, they do not bite correctly between them, they cause difficulty in keeping them clean, there is a risk of early loss, paradontosis problems and can cause headaches. Finally, they cause an unwanted aesthetic appearance.

Read more...

Periodontics.

If you feel inflammation or bleeding gums, this is the specialty that interests you. The culprit is always bacteria. There are many solutions, both on our part and yours. For an impeccable health of your gums, we recommend you review the way to take care of your dental hygiene. There are additional ways to maintain hygiene, and we will inform you of these if you visit us.
